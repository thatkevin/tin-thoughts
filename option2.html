Deliaphonic
A celebration of the life and work of Delia Derbyshire in her home city of Coventry. Born here in 1937, her pioneering work at the BBC Radiophonic Workshop shaped the sound of a generation. Deliaphonic brings her spirit back home.

About

Named after the warrior queen believed to have fought her final battle north of Coventry, Boudica Festival has run since 2017. As well as live events, the programme includes music video competitions, film screenings, and annual International Women's Day celebrations featuring West Midlands artists. Part of The Tin Music and Arts, Registered Charity No. 1152636.
