Deliaphonic
A celebration of the life and work of Delia Derbyshire in her home city of Coventry. Born here in 1937. Her pioneering work at the BBC Radiophonic Workshop shaped the sound of a generation.
Featuring Delia Derbyshire Day, the Delia's Sea of Sounds installation, film screenings of The Myths and the Legendary Tapes, archive concert videos, and past events from across the years.

Boudica
Festival · Est. 2017 · Coventry
Celebrating emerging women and non-binary musicians across all genres. Firmly on the frontline in the fight against inequality in the music industry. Part of The Tin Music and Arts.

Named after the warrior queen believed to have fought her final battle north of Coventry, Boudica has run since 2017. Events include live festivals, music video competitions, film screenings, and International Women's Day celebrations for West Midlands artists. Boudica also develops sound engineers, lighting technicians, and stage managers - not just performers.
