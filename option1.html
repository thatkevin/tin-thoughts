About

Deliaphonic
A celebration of the life and work of Delia Derbyshire in her home city of Coventry.

Delia Derbyshire was born in Coventry in 1937. A pioneering electronic composer, her work at the BBC Radiophonic Workshop shaped the sound of a generation. Deliaphonic brings her spirit back home.

Featuring live performances, Delia's Sea of Sounds installation, Delia Derbyshire Day, concert film screenings, and the documentary The Myths and the Legendary Tapes.

About

Boudica is inspired by the warrior queen believed to have fought her final battle north of Coventry. Since 2017, the festival has organised live events, music video competitions, film screenings, and International Women's Day celebrations featuring West Midlands acts. As well as putting women and non-binary artists onstage, Boudica develops a crew of sound engineers, lighting technicians, and stage managers - highlighting career opportunities beyond performing.
